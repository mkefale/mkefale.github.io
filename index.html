Charting My Own Path

When my friends and I weren't selected to lead a KWEST Trip, we made a pact that we would go on a trip to Southeast Asia ourselves. We were going to take full advantage of the four-week break between our internships and the start of second year to rack up as many Instagram-worthy travel photos as possible.

But when it came time to purchase our flights, I realized visiting five countries in three weeks was the opposite of the kind of vacation I wanted. I needed time to relax and recharge, and I felt especially disconnected from my friends outside Kellogg.

So I made my own plans to spend 10 days visiting friends in Seattle, Portland, and San Diego. Welcome to KWEST West Coast.
