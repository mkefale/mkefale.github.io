In Which I Forget to Take Pictures: San Diego

San Diego is where my documentarian skills fell apart. I was visiting my high school best friend, who I hadn't seen in years, and we were too busy catching up to bother with pictures. But at least the ones I have are pretty!

Over the Pacific

I dipped out of happy hour to catch the sun setting over the ocean. As you can see by the number of people on the beach, this was not an original idea on my part.

Looking Back

Technically, this picture wasn't taken in San Diego. I snapped through the airplane as we were heading back east, leaving the sun behind us. It's probably my favorite picture of the whole trip.
